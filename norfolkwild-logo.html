Usage rules
Always use
✓ The approved colour combinations shown above
✓ Minimum size: 80px wide for wordmark
✓ Clear space: at least half the mark-height on all sides
✓ The exact gold: #c9a84c (on dark) or #b8923a (on light)
✓ Playfair Display font for the wordmark
Never do this
✗ Change the font (no Comic Sans, no Arial)
✗ Stretch or distort the logo
✗ Use on busy photographic backgrounds
✗ Use colours outside the approved palette
✗ Add drop shadows or effects
